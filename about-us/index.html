Our Story

Journey With an Impact

Mental 360 was born out of Bright Shitemis mental health experience in 2016. His vision was to raise awareness, create safe spaces, and offer holistic support to underserved communities facing mental health challenges.

Mission

Strengthening psychosocial support systems for better mental health among the youth.

Vision

Healthy minds, healthy communities

Our Culture

Our Business Model

Mental 360 takes a not-for-profit social enterprise model. This is key to long-term sustainability and our effort to reduce donor reliance. Social impact community programs are donor-funded while our services are offered to corporate organizations at price value and to the general public on a sliding scale model (people get to pay what they can afford, and those who cannot afford are assessed and given services for free). Our definition of at-risk persons is not just about their socio-economic situation; we appreciate that mental health challenges affect everyone no matter their background, and we make our services available to all, meeting them at their point of need.

Our Approach

We are aware that well-informed and designed solutions often fail because they do not meet the needs of the target population or engage them in ways they can appreciate. At Mental 360, we have tailor-made our approach to our target audience to deepen our impact and increase our reach. These include:
  • Using storytelling, art (theater, poetry, and music), and experiential learning in delivery: This has proven to be a powerful tool in awareness creation as the engagement with our audience is authentic, relatable, and unforgettable.
  • Human-Centered Design: Our program design and implementation is participatory and flexible for constant iteration to best meet our audience's needs.
  • User-led interventions: Nothing for us, without us. We involve the various groups we work with in representation in the organization. The programs empower the participants to reproduce the training or knowledge in their own spaces.
  • Inclusive: We are non-discriminatory, so our services are open to all persons regardless of age, background, affiliation, or gender.
